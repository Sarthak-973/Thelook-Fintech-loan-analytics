# 📊 Unlocking Loan Insights | Cloud Data Analytics

**Prepared for:** TheLook Fintech Executive Team  

---

## 🔹 Overview
TheLook Fintech’s data team worked on a two-part analytics project to help the Treasury department understand important metrics such as cash flow, loan purpose, and borrower location.

As part of this project, a report and an interactive dashboard were created using Looker. The analysis focused on the total number of loans issued and other key metrics to help track loan performance and support better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the Fintech dataset to understand loan trends, including the total number of loans issued by day and year.
- Build an interactive dashboard to help the Treasury team easily track important loan performance metrics.

---

## 📊 Results

- The analysis shows that the total number of loans increased steadily from 2012 to 2019.
- A report (`loan_count_by_year`) was created to track yearly loan counts.
- Key metrics analyzed include:
  - Total outstanding loan balance
  - Borrower location
  - Customer income and home ownership

---

## 📈 Key Output

### Loan Count by Year (2012–2019)
- The number of loans increased over time, indicating business growth.
- Data was grouped using `issue_year` to track trends.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

The Looker dashboard includes:
- KPI: Total Outstanding Loan Amount
- Pie Chart: Loan Status Distribution
- Bar Chart: Top States by Loan Count
- Table: Top Customers by Income

---

## 🚀 Next Steps
